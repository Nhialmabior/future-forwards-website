Our Mission

To uplift and empower the people of Kakuma through education, youth engagement, peacebuilding, and gender equity, building a better future for all.

Our Programs

Education & Literacy

Enhancing access to quality education and literacy for children and adults in Kakuma.

Youth Empowerment

Supporting youth with leadership, vocational skills, and personal development programs.

Peacebuilding & Gender Equality

Fostering peaceful communities through dialogue and promoting women's empowerment.

About Us

Future Forwards Initiative is a community-based organization founded in Kakuma with a vision of building resilient, self-reliant, and peaceful communities through inclusive and sustainable programs.
